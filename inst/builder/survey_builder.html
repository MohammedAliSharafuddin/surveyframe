Collect responses in a Google Sheet, free and with no server. Do these steps in order:
  1. Create a blank Google Sheet and paste its link in the box below.
  2. Click Generate collector (.gs) to download the script. In your Sheet, open Extensions > Apps Script, delete any code there, and paste the downloaded file.
  3. In Apps Script, click Deploy > New deployment > Web app, set Who has access to Anyone, and copy the Web App URL it gives you.
  4. Paste that Web App URL in the last box, then click Export survey. Every response is now saved to your Sheet.
